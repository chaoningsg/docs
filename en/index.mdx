---
title: "Welcome to PromptDict.org!"
---

PromptDict is the public reference for AI prompting techniques and best practices. Our goal is to build an accessible and comprehensive wiki style knowledge base that helps both beginners and experts master AI communication through effective prompting.

Explore our growing collection of knowledge on PromptDict covering AI prompting fundamentals, context setting, instruction design, and practical examples you can apply to enhance your ability to communicate your intentions clearly to AI systems. Whether you are new to AI and looking to improve your everyday AI interactions, or an experienced user aiming to develop sophisticated prompting frameworks for professional applications, understanding prompting is key to unlocking the full potential of AI tools. PromptDict provides the knowledge and examples you need to succeed in the growing field of AI communication.

PromptDict is an ongoing project as AI technology evolves. To ensure PromptDict remains a valuable resource for AI prompting available, we are actively developing additional tools, refining existing entries, and incorporating community feedback. If you have suggestions, questions, or would like to contribute, please reach out to us at [info@promptdict.org](mailto:info@promptdict.org).
